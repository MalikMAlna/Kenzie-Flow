How does this work?

The Kenzie Flow is based on the Pomodoro technique.
Getting into the Kenzie Flow is quite simple:
You work for 25 minutes and then take a short 5 minute break to relax and step away from the screen for a moment.
Then you go back and do another 25 minute "flow" session. Once you have had 3 short breaks (and 4 flow sessions)
you take a long 15 minute break to let your mind relax.
